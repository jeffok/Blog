本文档介绍 ubuntu 1804 网卡配置 的相关内容。

## 多网卡静太路由配置

```yaml
network:
    version: 2
    renderer: networkd
    ethernets:
        ens3:
            dhcp4: no
            dhcp6: no
            addresses: [10.17.1.145/24]
            routes:
to: 10.0.0.0/8
              via: 10.17.1.254
to: 192.168.0.0/16
              via: 10.17.1.254
to: 172.16.0.0/12
              via: 10.17.1.254
        ens7:
            dhcp4: no
            dhcp6: no
            addresses: [1.2.3.4/24]
            gateway4: 1.2.3.4
            nameservers:
                addresses: [1.2.3.4,1.2.3.4]

```
