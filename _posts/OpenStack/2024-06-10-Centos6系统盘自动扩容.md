本文档介绍 Centos6系统盘自动扩容 的相关内容。

```bash
# centos6 系统盘自动扩容

1. 下载
wget https://github.com/flegmatik/linux-rootfs-resize/archive/master.ziphttp://dl.fedoraproject.org/pub/epel/6/x86_64/cloud-utils-growpart-0.27-10.el6.x86_64.rpm

2. 安装growpart
rpm -ivh cloud-utils-growpart-0.27-10.el6.x86_64.rpm
yum install parted
unzip master
cd linux-rootfs-resize-master
./install

https://docs.openstack.org/image-guide/centos-image.html
```
